Quick Facts

Covid-19 is a coronavirus that targets the respirtory system.

  • The virus is relatively large (400-500nm), so any mask can protect from it (not that a mask will protect your entire body).
  • The virus cannot live in tempuratures over 81 degrees farenheight.
  • The virus can live on metal surfaces and in clothes for up to 12 hours (any detergent will kill it in the wash). It will live on your hands for 10 minutes, with the risk of touching eyes/face in that time.
  • The virus is mostly transmitted through direct contact, but it can transmit through the air in close proximity.
  • Drinking water constantly is key. The water can carry the virus into your stomach where it will die. A dry throat brings risk of it reaching your respiratory system.
